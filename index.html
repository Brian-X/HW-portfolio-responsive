About Brian

Brian Ervin is an Austin-based Designer specializing in UI Design and related fields of graphics and photography, working with clients in the area and abroad.

"I want to create art that is unique, clever and authentic; something no one else has. Immaculate attention to detail from the big ideas to execution inspires awe."

Brian graduated from Southwestern University with a bachelors in studio art. He studied art history, architecture, design, and is a classically trained painter. He has 8 years experience branding small companies, products, creating collateral, designing websites, and supporting inventors with patent drawings.

In 2016, Brian graduated the Art Institute of Austin's web and interactive media program to pursue the field of UI Design. Brian's artwork has been distributed in the US, Germany, China, Poland, and Belarus.
